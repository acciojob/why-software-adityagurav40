Why I want to become a developer?

People choose to become developers for various reasons. Here are some common ones:

1. Passion for technology: Many individuals are fascinated by technology and how it works. Becoming a developer allows them to create, build, and innovate with technology every day.

2. Problem-solving skills: Developing software is often about finding solutions to complex problems. Developers enjoy the challenge of breaking down problems into manageable parts and creating a solution from scratch.

3. Creativity: Web and software development offer a lot of creative freedom. Developers can bring their ideas to life, build things from scratch, and create unique and innovative solutions.
